# The Old England Manor — Nantou, Taiwan

## Overview

The **Old England Manor** (老英格蘭莊園) is a luxury boutique hotel and iconic tourist destination nestled in the highlands of **Ren'ai Township, Nantou County, Taiwan**. Perched among the lush green mountains of central Taiwan near the world-famous Cingjing area, the manor delivers an extraordinary and unexpected taste of English countryside charm in the heart of Asia.

## Architecture & Design

The manor is celebrated for its stunning **Tudor-style architecture**, featuring:

- A **Gothic-style clock tower** whose bells chime at noon, 3 PM, and 6 PM
- Ornate **Baroque and Art Deco** decorative elements both inside and out
- Manicured English gardens with sculpted hedgerows and flower beds
- Authentic period furnishings ranging from **Rococo to Art Deco** styles

The striking European silhouette set against mist-covered Taiwanese mountains makes the manor one of the most photographed landmarks in Nantou County.

## Dining

### Virginal Restaurant
The on-site fine-dining restaurant offers a menu inspired by European cuisine, crafted using fresh local Taiwanese ingredients. Guests can enjoy:

- **Set and buffet breakfasts** with a wide selection of Western and local dishes
- **Afternoon tea on the terrace** — one of the most beloved experiences at the manor, with panoramic views of the surrounding mountains
- Candlelit dinners in a richly decorated dining hall
