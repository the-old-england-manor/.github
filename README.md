# The Old England Manor — Nantou, Taiwan

> *A castle on a cloud, where Elizabethan England meets the misty mountains of central Taiwan.*

---

## Overview

**The Old England Manor** (老英格蘭莊園) is a distinctive luxury boutique hotel and resort nestled in the highlands of **Ren'ai Township, Nantou County, Taiwan**. Designed in the authentic style of an **English Tudor manor**, the property is celebrated for its striking Gothic-inspired clock tower, grand stone façades, and opulent interiors that transport guests to the elegance of Elizabethan England — all set against the breathtaking backdrop of Taiwan's lush central mountain range.

Whether you are seeking a romantic retreat, a one-of-a-kind themed escape, or simply a tranquil base for exploring Nantou's natural wonders, The Old England Manor offers an unforgettable experience unlike anywhere else in Asia.

---

## Architecture & Atmosphere

The manor's design is a faithful homage to classic English Tudor architecture:

- **Exterior:** Stone façades, timber-framed details, and a soaring Gothic-style clock tower whose bells ring at regular intervals, reinforcing the sense of stepping back in time.
- **Interior:** Lavishly decorated rooms featuring period furnishings spanning **Rococo**, **Victorian**, and **Art Deco** styles, ornate chandeliers, regal patterned wallpapers, and an extensive collection of English artefacts and statues.
- **Grounds:** Manicured English gardens, decorative sculptures, and panoramic views of the surrounding misty mountains create a fairy-tale atmosphere that guests frequently describe as *magical*.

## Rooms & Suites

Each room and suite at The Old England Manor is individually styled and luxuriously appointed:

## Location & Nearby Attractions

Situated in the highland area of **Ren'ai Township**, the manor is perfectly positioned for exploring Nantou's most iconic natural and cultural destinations:

- 🌾 **Cingjing Farm** — only an 8-minute drive; famous for its rolling green pastures and European-style scenery.
- 🏔 **Hehuanshan National Forest Recreation Area** — a stunning alpine landscape offering hiking and breathtaking views.
- 🌊 **Sun Moon Lake** — one of Taiwan's most celebrated scenic landmarks, known for its serene waters and surrounding mountains.
- 🌸 **Small Swiss Garden** — picturesque gardens with European-inspired landscaping.
- 🌉 **Cingjing Skywalk** — a thrilling elevated walkway with panoramic mountain panoramas.
